Supporting COMAT with Every Fidget

Every time you buy a fidget, part of that money goes directly to COMAT — a nonprofit helping martial artists travel to events, training, and competitions.

COMAT is a organization committed to helping martial artists reach their goals by removing financial barriers. We’re proud to contribute to their mission.

About the Creator

These fidgets are created by a designer and some designs from others on makerworld @Zabois29, @YANGYANG0710, and @the3Dimagineer.

What began as a kid doing martial arts for fun turned into friendships and support for the martial arts community, Inspired by a love of martial arts and 3D printing, each fidget is made with quality and care.
